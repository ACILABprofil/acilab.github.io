    He is a dynamic expert known for leading successful participatory budgeting projects, showcasing his deep understanding of Democratic innovation and collective intelligence methodologies. Currently a graduate researcher in Collective Intelligence at the University Mohammed VI Polytechnic (UM6P), Rabat, Morocco, George holds a master's degree in Big Data Engineering from Sup Management, Intercontinental University in Fes, Morocco, and a bachelor's degree in Economics from Mohammed V University - Souissi, Rabat, Morocco. He is a recipient of the Ibn Roch Excellence Merit Award Scholarship and holds a Certificate in Project Management from the Institute of Professional and Executive Development in the UK. At ACILAB, George champions democratic innovations, and design thinking and integrates cutting-edge digital platforms for groundbreaking ideas. His role advocates for a balance between participatory and deliberative democracy for effective governance, positioning ACILAB as a catalyst for democratic Innovation in Africa. With expertise in Development Planning in Ghana and skilled data analysis, George significantly contributed to drafting the District Medium-Term Development Plan and Monitoring and evaluation of Projects. His consulting extends to both Government and private industries, including facilitating training programs at the Guinea Aluminum Corporation in Guinea, Conakry. As a lead consultant at Adepts Institute of Research in Accra, George excels in mapping business and governance problems to data analytics solutions and applying statistical and econometrics models to derive meaningful insights. His profile reflects a comprehensive blend of academic achievements, professional expertise, and a track record of impactful contributions across various domains.
